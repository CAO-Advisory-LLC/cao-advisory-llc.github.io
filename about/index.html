Principal
Gary Cao

As an advisor to boards and CEOs, Gary is a C-suite AI and data science executive with 20+ years of experience in AI analytics and data, innovation and growth, and risk governance.

He currently advises board members and CEOs of multiple private equity portfolio companies, with annual revenues of $100+ million and 400+ employees.

As a Chief Data and Analytics / AI Officer (CDAO) across various industries, Gary is quick to spot the gaps and opportunities for the Boards and the CEOs to utilize and scale up AI.

Gary has founded 8 internal startups in data and analytics at 5 large public companies (Cardinal Health, Elevance Health, Chase, Bank of America, Equifax), 3 private companies (CFSI/Chase, IXI/Equifax, US Venture), 1 retailer (Express), and 1 nonprofit (Akron Children's), with revenues from $40 million to $120+ billion.

As a Board Director at Samaritan Counseling Center (Wisconsin), he is Business Strategy Committee chair and Audit Committee member. He is an advisory partner at Rev1 Ventures (tech startup studio) and an expert advisor at International Institute for Analytics (think tank on enterprise AI strategy).

As a member of Private Directors Association (PDA), Gary volunteers at its national Board Composition Committee (vice chair 2026), AI national special interest group (co-lead 2025-), and Cleveland Chapter membership committee (co-chair 2025).

Gary has written numerous thought leadership articles on Private Company Director magazine, PDA Blog, Chief Data Officer Magazine, and AIM Research. He is a frequent speaker and panelist at conferences and podcasts focused on AI, Analytics, and Data enterprise journeys.

Gary has earned three master's degrees (listed above) and one bachelor's degree: Bachelor of Economics (Peking University).
